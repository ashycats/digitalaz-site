Frequently asked

Good to know
Why work with Digital A-Z?+

We make the process approachable, collaborative, and efficient — blending creativity with strategy to turn your rough ideas into polished, high-performing Squarespace websites. As a Squarespace Circle Gold Member, every site meets the highest standards for design, performance, and functionality, with access to insider resources, extended trials, and discounts.

What services do you offer?+

Digital A-Z specializes in custom Squarespace website design. Whether you need a brand-new site or a complete refresh, I create modern, responsive websites that perform well, look great, and are easy to manage — each one tailored to your goals and built to grow with your business.

How does the process work?+

We start with discovery and strategy to define your goals, audience, and content structure. Then a sitemap and wireframes, followed by design mockups. Once you approve the design, I build your site on Squarespace, refine it through testing and revisions, and launch. Most projects take 4 to 8 weeks, depending on content and revisions.

Will my site be mobile-friendly and editable?+

Yes. Every website is fully responsive, so it looks and works great on mobile, tablet, and desktop. After launch, I'll train you to manage and update your content yourself — no technical or coding knowledge required.

What's included in every package?+

Every package includes full Squarespace account setup, a customized template with professionally designed pages, domain and hosting configuration, a custom contact form, and social media integration. New websites also get a 20% discount on their first Squarespace subscription.

What package add-ons are available?+

Add-ons can be included with any package or purchased separately: SEO optimization, ongoing maintenance, troubleshooting and support, content development, copy editing, and graphic design.
